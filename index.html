Our Key Features

AI-Powered Case Archive

Fetch case details, generate AI summary, download PDF copies seamlessly.

Know More

Our AI-Powered Case Archive revolutionizes legal case management by automatically retrieving user and case details from trusted sources using a case or FIR number. Leveraging advanced AI technology, it aggregates comprehensive data, generates an insightful summary highlighting key points, and allows users to download the entire case file as a PDF. This streamlined process not only saves time but also ensures accuracy and efficiency in managing legal documents, empowering lawyers to quickly access and review vital case information for improved decision-making and enhanced client service.

AI-Powered Case Search

Aggregate user data, fetch recent cases, interactive chat for modifications.

Know More

Our AI Legal Insights tool leverages state-of-the-art technology to consolidate user details from multiple trusted sources while retrieving the most recent and relevant case law linked to your specific legal matters. By intelligently curating up-to-date case precedents and legal documentation, this feature streamlines your research process and enhances case strategy formulation. Additionally, an interactive chat function empowers lawyers to engage with the system in real time, refining and modifying drafts to better align with evolving legal arguments and client needs. This comprehensive approach ensures efficiency, accuracy, and informed decision-making throughout your legal workflow

Intelligent Draft Assistant

Retrieve client details, auto-draft legal documents, chat for seamless revisions.

Know More

Our platform leverages state-of-the-art AI to gather comprehensive user details from various sources, streamlining the legal drafting process. It automatically compiles essential information to create initial draft documents tailored for lawyers. In addition, an interactive chat feature enables real-time modifications, ensuring the draft meets precise legal standards. This innovative tool not only reduces manual effort but also enhances accuracy and efficiency, empowering legal professionals to deliver well-structured, comprehensive legal documents with ease.
